Given a chessboard, of size NxN .
(Where N is number >0, denoting number of small squares of side-1unit that can fit along one side of the chessboard)

Problem Statement: Compute the total number of squares and rectangles in the chessboard.

Constraints: 2 <= N <= 50

Input Format:   Single line input, denoting N.
Output Format: <number of rectangles> <number of squares>
